Goals

The primary goals of Townsville Community Music Centre is to add new members to its existing fleet. TCMC strives to sell tickets specially to general public and aims to fosters the highest standards of music in the community by organizing concerts for local performers or visitors and encourage them to let music centre organize it.

Success Evaluation

TCMC has developed some performance metrics on the basis of which the success of this web based project will be evaluated. Some of them are listed below:

  • Traffic of page visitors is one of the major metric for success evaluation.
  • Conversion rate from number of visitors to number of new members through sign-up forms available, downloads and submissions.
  • Increment on the number of tickets booked on the web.
  • How well TCMC is trending on social media sharing its contents throughout the web.

Target Audience

  • Audience ranges particularly from all age groups but involvement of childrens are predominated by parents and family menbers.
  • TCMC specializes on diversity, meaning we cater audiences with specialized tastes including classical, jazz, ethnic music, etc.
